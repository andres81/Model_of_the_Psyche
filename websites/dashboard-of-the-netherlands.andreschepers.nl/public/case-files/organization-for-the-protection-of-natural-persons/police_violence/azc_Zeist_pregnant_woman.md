## Introduction

Police violence was used against a pregnant woman in the AZC Zeist. The 
police has now decided not to share the internal audit and results of the 
investigation regarding the incident.

In a democracy, we the people, should be able to decide what is right and 
what is wrong regarding the use of police violence. The police might have a 
monopoly on the use of violence; however, this does not mean that they have 
a monopoly on the decision what violence is allowed. Take the water cannon 
incident in Eindhoven, that was police brutality and a complete misuse of 
violence allowed by the police in certain circumstances.

I will have to write a letter to the police midden Nederland and communicate 
with the political parties in The Netherlands, to ask if they are going to 
defend our democracy or not. Irrespective of political preference, we all 
have to stand together if it comes to protecting our democracy. And that 
comes in part in the form of preventing a police state from forming. 
Religious ideologies that are developing, banality of evil that spreads like 
mold, 
that need to be stopped. This is done first by checking 
organizations like you check your home for mold and bacteria.
The police organization not allowing us, we the people, from checking their 
policies and procedures, is a first step in loosing grip on protecting and 
maintaining our democracy.

These times are reminiscent of the '30s of the 20th century, unfortunately.
